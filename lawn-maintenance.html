Lawn Maintenance That Keeps Your Yard Sharp

Reliable mowing, edging, blowing, and seasonal cleanup — we keep your investment looking its best. Limited availability to ensure every client gets quality attention.

What's Included

Every maintenance visit covers the full set of tasks needed to keep your lawn looking professional.

  • Lawn mowing — all areas, consistent cut height
  • String trimming around beds, fences, and structures
  • Edging along sidewalks, driveways, and hardscape
  • Blowing clippings off driveways, walks, and patios
  • Debris clearing from lawn surface
  • Seasonal cleanup (spring & fall)

Why Jaramillo Landscape?

We offer lawn maintenance on a limited, selective basis — we don't take on every property that calls. That means each client on our schedule gets real attention, not a rushed visit from a crew that's trying to hit a daily quota. When we show up, we do the job right.

Our crews mow at the correct height for the grass type and season, edge cleanly every visit, and blow all hard surfaces before leaving. We don't skip steps when the schedule gets tight.

Because we do the hardscape, irrigation, and landscape installs as well, we understand your whole yard — not just the lawn. If we notice an irrigation head that's been hit, a drainage concern, or a plant that needs attention, we'll tell you.

Our Process

Consistent, professional service every visit — no shortcuts, no excuses.

1

Initial Walk-Through & Schedule Setup

We walk the property, note any specific instructions, set a mowing height, and establish your service day and frequency.

2

Mowing

All lawn areas are mowed at the correct height, alternating direction each visit to reduce compaction and improve appearance.

3

Trimming & Edging

String trimming around all obstacles, then clean edging along all hardscape borders. This is what gives a lawn a manicured, professional look.

4

Blowing

All clippings, debris, and dust are blown off driveways, sidewalks, and patios before we leave — the property is as clean as when we arrived.

5

Seasonal Adjustments

Service frequency is adjusted by season — more frequent in peak growing season (May–September), reduced in shoulder months.

Service Frequency

Weekly or Bi-Weekly

Most residential clients are served weekly during peak season (May–September) and bi-weekly in spring/fall. We adjust based on your lawn's growth rate and preferences.

Cost Factors

  • Total lawn square footage
  • Service frequency (weekly vs. bi-weekly)
  • Property complexity (slopes, obstacles)
  • Add-on services (seasonal cleanup, edging)
  • Location within our service area
