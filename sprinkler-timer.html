What's Included

We handle the full controller swap or upgrade — from removing the old unit to walking you through the new one.

  • Old controller removal & disposal
  • New timer or smart controller installation
  • Zone wiring reconnection
  • Schedule programming (days, times, run lengths)
  • WiFi setup & app connection (smart controllers)
  • All zones tested after installation
  • Owner walk-through & training

Why Jaramillo Landscape?

A controller that's hard to program or unreliable is a source of constant frustration — and often leads to overwatering or underwatering because the schedule gets set wrong. We make the installation process smooth and leave you knowing exactly how to use your new system.

We install and program Rachio, Rain Bird, Hunter, Orbit B-Hyve, and other major brands. We're honest about which controller is right for your number of zones and comfort level with technology. Not everyone needs a fully connected smart controller, and we won't upsell you to features you won't use.

For smart controllers, we connect the WiFi, download the app, link weather data to your account, and make sure everything is working before we leave.

Our Process

A clean, documented installation so you always know how to operate your system.

1

Assess Current Controller & Wiring

We check the current controller, document which wire goes to which zone, and identify any wiring issues before removing the old unit.

2

Remove Old Controller

The old controller is carefully removed and wires are labeled for easy reconnection to the new unit.

3

Install New Controller

The new controller is mounted and secured. All zone wires are connected to the correct terminals.

4

Program Schedule

We program a watering schedule based on your zone types, plant needs, and local watering days or restrictions.

5

WiFi & App Setup (Smart Controllers)

For smart controllers, we connect to your WiFi, download and configure the app on your phone, and link weather station data.

6

Test All Zones & Walk-Through

Every zone is run from the new controller to confirm proper operation. We walk you through the interface so you can manage it confidently.
