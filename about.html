About Jaramillo Landscape

Locally owned, locally invested — we've been building beautiful yards in Treasure Valley for over 15 years.

Our Story

Jaramillo Landscape was founded right here in Treasure Valley with one simple belief: every homeowner deserves an outdoor space they're proud of. What started as a small crew taking on residential yards has grown into a full-service landscaping company with 15+ years of experience and hundreds of completed projects across the valley.

We're not a national franchise. We live here, we work here, and we care deeply about the neighborhoods we serve. That local commitment means we show up on time, we communicate clearly, and we stand behind every job we complete.

Whether you need a same-day sprinkler repair or a full backyard transformation, you'll get the same dedicated crew and the same honest, hardworking service every time.
