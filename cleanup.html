What's Included

Whether you need a quick cleanup or a full property prep, we handle every step of the job.

  • Debris removal (branches, rocks, old materials)
  • Sod & dead vegetation clearing
  • Overgrown brush & weed removal
  • Yard grading & leveling
  • Pre-construction site preparation
  • Property edge clearing
  • Hauling & disposal of all removed material
  • Light regrading for improved drainage

Why Jaramillo Landscape?

A proper cleanup isn't just hauling stuff away — it's preparing the canvas for everything you want to build next. We work quickly and efficiently without cutting corners, and we leave every site cleaner than we found it.

Grading is one of the most underappreciated parts of landscaping. Improper slope is the root cause of most water damage, soggy spots, and drainage failures. Our crew knows how to read a yard and create the right slope to protect your home and landscaping investments for years.

We haul everything off-site and take care of disposal — you don't have to worry about where anything goes.

Our Process

Efficient, thorough, and respectful of your property at every step.

1

Property Walk & Assessment

We walk the entire site with you, identify what's going and what's staying, and note any grading or drainage needs.

2

Debris Sorting & Removal

Branches, rocks, old material, and debris are sorted and removed systematically, starting with the largest material first.

3

Vegetation Clearing

Sod, dead plants, overgrown brush, and unwanted ground cover are cleared down to bare soil.

4

Grading & Leveling

We grade the soil to achieve proper slope away from structures and level low spots that collect water.

5

Haul-Away & Final Inspection

All material is loaded and hauled off-site. We do a final walkthrough with you to confirm everything meets expectations.

Typical Timeframe

1–2 Days

Most yard cleanups are completed in a single day. Larger properties or extensive grading work may require a second day.
